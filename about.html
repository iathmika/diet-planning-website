About

DietGuru is an online nutrient-based diet planner which primarily aims at improving your health by providing diet recommendations as well as reminders based on your height, weight, age, gender, lifestyle and other such factors. We at DietGuru, strive to provide you with a personalised experience which makes living a healthy lifestyle easier than ever.
It's undeniable that a well-balanced diet goes hand in hand with a healthy lifestyle. What you choose to eat, and what you choose not to eat, are the factors in warding off many illnesses and diseases. Food choices make a huge impact on how you feel today, tomorrow and what the future holds in terms of promoting and maintaining good health.
DietGuru's extensive catalogue of diet plans and recommendations contain a multitude of reliable diet plans for each type of user. Be it weight control diets, or a detox diet, or a predominantly vegetarian diet, DietGuru has it all!
